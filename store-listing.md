## Short Description (132 chars max)

## Detailed Description
Stop staring at a blank LinkedIn post. LinkedIn Post Generator uses AI to write engaging posts and smart replies in seconds — so you can focus on building your network, not agonizing over every word.

HOW IT WORKS
1. Click the extension icon on any page
2. Choose "New post" or "Smart reply"
3. Enter your topic (or paste the post you're replying to)
4. Pick a tone and length
5. Get a polished draft instantly — copy and post

6 POST TONES
- Thought leader — authoritative, insightful takes
- Casual — like texting a smart friend
- Storytelling — hook readers with narrative
- Contrarian — bold, unconventional perspectives
- Educational — teach something useful
- Motivational — inspire without the cringe

5 REPLY TONES
- Supportive — validate and build on their point
- Add value — extend with examples or resources
- Respectful disagree — challenge with class
- Curious — ask thoughtful follow-up questions
- Witty — sharp and clever

SMART REPLY FROM LINKEDIN
Click the "AI Reply" button that appears on LinkedIn posts to capture the text, then open the extension to generate a tailored reply.

FREE & PRO PLANS
- Free: 3 generations per day
- Pro ($7.99/mo): Unlimited generations

No hashtag spam. No corporate jargon. No "I'm excited to announce." Just authentic, human-sounding content that gets engagement.
